Your Place in the Story

Introduction

You've read the whole story of redemption, haven't you? How does all this concern you? What's your place in the story of redemption? It's important to note that all this, God did for you. You are the center of the redemption story—you are the object of God's affection. Is it not time to play your role in this story?

You Were in God's Heart

You were in God's heart from the beginning. The cross was for you. The empty tomb, the heavenly ministry, the soon return— all for you. Redemption isn’t just a theory or a doctrine. It’s a love story. And you are the beloved.

“Return unto Me; for I have redeemed thee” (Isaiah 44:22).

Whatever your past, however far you’ve wandered, Jesus is calling you home. You were created for more—for freedom, joy, and purpose in Christ.

“To every soul Christ is saying: ‘I have a place for you at My side. Come and learn of Me.’” — Ministry of Healing, p. 85

This is your time. Let your life be a living testimony of redemption. Be part of this great movement that points the world to Jesus. Your story can help others find their place in God's story too.
