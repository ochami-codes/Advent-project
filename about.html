About Second Advent Ministries

Proclaiming Christ. Preparing for His Soon Return.

Second Advent Ministries is a Christian digital ministry committed to spreading the everlasting gospel of Jesus Christ, with a special emphasis on the three angels’ messages of Revelation 14. Rooted in the historic Seventh-day Adventist faith, we exist to uplift Christ, call people to repentance, and prepare souls for His soon return.

We believe in the power of digital evangelism to reach hearts across the world. Through Bible-based content, spiritual resources, and practical Christian living guides, we aim to help people experience true conversion, grow in grace, and stand firm in these last days.

Our Vision

To see lives transformed through the preaching of the present truth and to help prepare a people ready to meet Jesus at His Second Coming.

Our Mission

  • Share Bible truth with clarity and love
  • Inspire daily personal revival and reform
  • Equip believers to be faithful witnesses in their homes, churches, and communities

What We Believe

  • The Bible is the inspired Word of God and our foundation for all faith and practice
  • Jesus Christ is our Savior, High Priest, and soon-coming King
  • Salvation is by grace through faith, and true obedience is the fruit of a converted life
  • God's Ten Commandments, including the Sabbath, are still binding and relevant today
  • The health message is part of God's plan to restore His image in man
  • The three angels’ messages are a divine call to come out of Babylon and worship the Creator in spirit and truth

How We Serve

  • YouTube Bible teachings and devotionals
  • Free Bible study resources and guides
  • Social media evangelism and digital outreach
  • Practical Christian living messages and testimonies
