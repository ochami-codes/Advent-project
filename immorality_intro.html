Sex and Sexual Immorality

“Man shall leave his father and his mother and be joined to his wife, and the two shall become one flesh.” This union, of course, is expressed through sexual intimacy. Paul emphasizes in 1 Corinthians that sexual relations create a form of oneness—even with a prostitute—highlighting the sacred bond sex is meant to establish within marriage.

God’s design for sex is not merely physical; it is intended to unite husband and wife, fostering emotional, spiritual, and relational oneness. Yet, just as with every perfect gift from God—such as the Sabbath—the enemy has created a counterfeit, twisting God’s holy design for sex into something perverse and destructive.

In our world today, sexual activity often ignores boundaries, age, or marital status, resulting in bitter consequences that harm individuals, families, and our relationship with God.

In this series, we will explore worldly sexual practices that defy God’s will, examine their tragic consequences, and learn how to honor and appreciate sex as the sacred gift God intended.
