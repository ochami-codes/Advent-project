Christian Standards and End-Time Remnant

Introduction

In the last days, God calls a people to stand firm on His unchanging truth—His law and gospel. This faithful group, known as the end-time remnant, is characterized by obedience to God’s commandments and a vibrant relationship with Jesus.

“Here is the patience of the saints: here are they that keep the commandments of God, and the faith of Jesus.”
Revelation 14:12
“The remnant church as the last light of truth shining in a dark world, called to prepare people for Christ’s soon return.”
The Great Controversy, p. 613

The Importance of God’s Law

Christian standards are rooted in God’s holy law, summarized in the Ten Commandments. These commandments reveal God’s character and His will for our lives. Jesus Himself affirmed their ongoing importance.

“Till heaven and earth pass, one jot or one tittle shall in no wise pass from the law.”
Matthew 5:18
“The law of God is the foundation of all true education and the standard of all right living.”
Education, p. 131

Obedience to God’s law is not legalism but love in action.

Holiness and Separation from the World

The remnant church is called to holiness and separation from worldly practices that contradict God’s standards. Paul urged believers to separate themselves from worldly influences.

“Come out from among them, and be ye separate.”
2 Corinthians 6:17
“The remnant people are to be distinguished from the world by their godly life.”
Testimonies for the Church, Vol. 5, p. 214

True holiness shines as a witness to God’s transforming power.

Living the Faith of Jesus

The remnant’s faith is not passive but active and vibrant. It involves trusting Christ daily, overcoming sin, and witnessing to others. Faith is linked closely with obedience.

“The faith of Jesus will be revealed in the remnant church by works of righteousness.”
The Great Controversy, p. 624

This faith keeps the remnant steadfast amid trials and persecution.

The Mission of the Remnant

God’s remnant church has a worldwide mission to proclaim the three angels’ messages of Revelation 14. This includes warning of judgment, calling people to worship God alone, and preparing them for Jesus’ soon return.

“The message of the third angel is to be proclaimed with a loud voice, a message of warning and of hope to the world.”
Testimonies for the Church, Vol. 6, p. 19

Upholding Christian standards is essential to this mission, as the remnant demonstrates God’s truth in word and deed.

Conclusion

Christian standards are not burdensome rules but God’s loving guideposts that prepare His people for Christ’s return. The end-time remnant stands as a faithful witness, reflecting God’s law and the faith of Jesus in a world that increasingly rejects both.

“The remnant people are to be clothed with the righteousness of Christ.”
The Great Controversy, p. 611

Let us live faithfully, shining as lights in the last days.
