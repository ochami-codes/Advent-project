The Power of the NEWSTART Lifestyle

“Whether therefore ye eat, or drink, or whatsoever ye do, do all to the glory of God.” – 1 Corinthians 10:31

In our modern world filled with stress, fast food, and lifestyle diseases, God has given us a simple yet powerful blueprint for health. It's called NEWSTART, an acronym that stands for Nutrition, Exercise, Water, Sunlight, Temperance, Air, Rest, and Trust in God. Rooted in both Scripture and science, this eight-part health plan is not just about living longer, it's about living abundantly in body, mind, and spirit.

Nutrition is the foundation of life. God gave us the original diet in Genesis 1:29, fruits, grains, nuts, and later vegetables.

“Grains, fruits, nuts, and vegetables...impart a strength, a power of endurance, and a vigor of intellect that are not afforded by a more complex and stimulating diet.” – Counsels on Diet and Foods, p. 81

Exercise is God’s natural energizer. Regular movement strengthens the heart, supports mental clarity, and boosts immunity. Even Jesus walked from village to village, showing us the power of active living.

“Walking, in all cases where it is possible, is the best remedy for diseased bodies.” – Ministry of Healing, p. 261

Water is a simple but powerful agent of health. Drinking enough pure water flushes toxins, supports organ function, and prevents dehydration. Jesus Himself used water in healing and ministry.

Sunlight is God's natural vitamin D supply. Moderate sunlight exposure lifts mood, strengthens bones, and supports immunity.

“Rooms that are not exposed to sunlight become damp... disease germs flourish in dark places.” – Ministry of Healing, p. 274

Temperance means balance—avoiding harmful things and using good things moderately. This includes abstaining from alcohol, tobacco, and drugs, and not overindulging in food, work, or even sleep.

“True temperance teaches us to dispense entirely with everything hurtful.” – Temperance, p. 138

Air and Rest are gifts we often overlook. Fresh air boosts brain function, and Sabbath rest renews the soul. God Himself rested on the seventh day, giving us a divine example.

Finally, Trust in God is the crown of the NEWSTART plan. Without it, true healing is incomplete.

“The love which Christ diffuses through the whole being is a vitalizing power.” – Ministry of Healing, p. 115

The NEWSTART lifestyle is not just health advice—it’s a return to God’s original plan for our well-being. It blends divine wisdom with scientific validation, offering healing and hope to a sick world. As we embrace these simple remedies, we draw closer to the Healer Himself.

“Come unto me... and I will give you rest.” – Matthew 11:28

Let us choose today to cooperate with heaven in preserving our health—for God’s glory and our mission’s success.
